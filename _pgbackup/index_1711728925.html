Frequently Asked Questions

Membership service

What's the maximum number of requests allowed?

Subscribe and you can ask for as many designs and features as you need; they will be delivered one by one.

How fast will I receive my designs?

Yes, requests typically take about 48 hours to be delivered, but this depends on the complexity of the request; some requests may take much longer. It's a job based on human hours.

What platforms and software do you use?

Most requests are designed using Figma and developed with Webflow.

Can I request changes if the design doesn't appeal to me?

No problem! We will make adjustments to the design until you feel it's right.

Are there any designs or features you don't cover?

We cover regular Webflow development; advanced custom code or solutions need to be negotiated separately.

Caribe Sky specifically excludes services related to 3D modeling, the generation of animated graphics (such as GIFs), the creation of documents (medical forms, etc.), complex packaging designs, and large-format print design (magazines, books, etc.).

Are there any extra costs?

For additional features that incur extra costs, such as Memberstack or Zapier, the client must pay the subscription fees or cost for those services, and we will happily integrate them.

Is there a refund policy in case the service doesn't meet my expectations?

Because our services are of high quality and involve significant human effort, we do not issue refunds.

one time payment

How long until my website is ready?

It depends on its size. Typically, a 5-page site is ready in 2 weeks, while a larger 10-20-page one might take 3-5 weeks. But don't worry, if you have a deadline, we are here to help you.

Are there any surprise fees I should be aware of?

Special features like custom widgets, premium plugins, and integrations are billed hourly, plus you'll need to pay any costs for the plugins or services directly to their providers. Typically, these aren't necessary, and we're here to assist you in keeping expenses minimal.

Will it be hard to make my own updates/changes?

Nope! I make all my websites with the client in mind by making the website easy and intuitive to edit, update, and add content. If you're not confident in your technical capabilities, we can sit down for a 1-on-1 tutorial.

What happens when I get my website? Does it require maintenance?

Your website should only need a couple minutes of maintenance a month. It's common for people to even leave their website for 5 months and it be okay. All your website would need is plugin and framework updates to keep it running securely. I've also written on article on a websites maintenance to help clients.

Do I get revisions?

After I’ve made and completed the website, we make updates and perfect the website based on your feedback. After this, we launch the website and make it live!
